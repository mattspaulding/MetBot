Other Donation Opportunities

Monthly Giving

Special friends who give regularly are the backbone of the Ministries. Because we can count on your gift each month, we are able to provide critically needed meals, and a place for children like Billy to call home. Give a monthly gift.

Shop Our Gift Catalog

Shop our gift catalog to provide struggling families with items like diapers, household items and food. Your donation will be significant because it will help a family avoid having to choose between paying rent and buying food. Shop the gift catalog.

Donate Goods

When people donate new or lightly used goods, it makes every dollar go further. Bottom line: We can help more people. So, if you don’t have cash, but have clothes, or maybe some household items, you can still make a huge difference. Find out how to donate new or lightly used goods.

Corporate Giving

You’ve got a lot to give! Your organization can employ its own unique approach to philanthropy and support our work through volunteerism, strategic giving and sharing professional skills. Corporate partners, large and small, work with Metropolitan Ministries to help poor, hungry, and homeless men, women and children in the Tampa Bay area. Learn more about corporate giving.

Planned Giving

Are you looking to leave a legacy? Do you want to bless families beyond your lifetime. A planned gift makes this possible. The most common type of planned gift is a bequest in a will, but there are many ways you can give. Learn how to build a legacy through planned giving.
