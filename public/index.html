Meet Metropolitan Ministries

Comprehensive Care for Poor and Homeless Families

Food

Hope starts with a meal. We provide 1.3 million meals a year.

Housing

Safe shelter creates security. We provide 174,639 nights off the street each year for families.

Children

Intervention breaks the cycle of homelessness. We care for 1,010 children each year.

Life Skills

Empowerment leads to self-sufficiency. 98% of our graduates have not returned to homelessness.

Resources

Our Proven Approach

1. Alleviate Suffering 2. Promote Dignity 3. Instill Self-Sufficiency
It’s hard to hang on to hope when you’re hungry or sleeping on the streets. We reach out to meet the most urgent needs of poor, homeless or hungry men, women and children: food, safety and housing.

View Client Success Rates

We provide men, women and children with keys to nutrition and healthy living. Through education, counseling and job training, we help them make the most of their minds. And we focus on the spirit inside them by offering forgiveness, encouragement and connection to God.

View Client Success Rates

As a family gets back on their feet, we give them back responsibility to care for themselves. Once they have stable housing, employment and a good education, we encourage community involvement and giving back.

View Client Success Rates

Education and job-skills training are long-term solutions for families. We offer GED classes so that our adult residents and anyone in the local community can finish high school.

Temporary Housing

Six months. That’s how long a typical family spends on the street. Can you imagine? Temporary housing and the right support can get a family back on their feet again.

Learn More

Early Childhood Education Center

PromiseLand Early Childhood Education Center is where children of our residents on site and in the local community are cared for during the day while their parents are working towards building a better life for their family.

Health & Wellness
